The simulation is discussed in the paper in the section on phonetics. Greek [ts] and [dz] have been controversial and are the subject of many studies; see <a href="https://docs.wixstatic.com/ugd/7b05fe_5116cbcc20c7440b87a2737647d0e3b6.pdf">Arvaniti 2007</a> for a great overview of that literature. Our learner does not find any complex segments, supporting the view that [ts] and [dz] are clusters, just like [ps] and [ks] are in Greek.<br>The Greek corpus is a lemma list from the <a
href="http://web-corpora.net/GreekCorpus/search/">Corpus of Modern Greek</a>. It was provided to us by <a href="https://iling.spb.ru/people/kisilier.html">Maxim Kisilier</a>. The transcriptions were generated from orthography via a script available <a href="https://github.com/gouskova/transcribers/tree/master/greek">here</a>.

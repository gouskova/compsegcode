This dataset was prepared by <a href="https://wp.nyu.edu/szaboildi/">Ildi Emese Szabó</a> from the Szótár AdatBázis 1.0 dictionary. The process of cleaning and transcribing is described <a href="https://github.com/gouskova/transcribers/tree/master/hungarian">here</a>.
